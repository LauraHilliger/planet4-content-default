Welcome to Planet 4

Congratulations! you managed to install planet4. Find all the project documentation on our wiki.

Planet 4 is the codename for the complete redesign of Greenpeace.org. Built on Wordpress as content management system (CMS), Planet 4 will be used to create and manage Greenpeace websites around the globe.

This site includes all you need to know when joining the project as a staff member or volunteer, and will you get up to speed without scanning in the zillion documents, Medium posts and Jira tickets which compose such a huge project.
